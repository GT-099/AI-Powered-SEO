# How to Build a Representative AI Search Prompt Library for Better AI Visibility Measurement
- Author: Aleyda Solis

## Summary
A detailed guide on building AI search prompt libraries to measure brand visibility
across AI platforms (ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, Perplexity, etc.). Covers:
- Common mistakes (using generic/default prompt sets, blending platforms, over-relying on branded prompts)
- A framework for structuring prompts by product line, audience, market, journey stage, and buyer constraints
- How to build a "prompt matrix" and tag prompts with metadata for analysis
- A measurement protocol (multiple runs, consistent sessions, platform-specific tracking)
- Turning findings into optimization actions (content gaps, representation fixes, competitor positioning)
- A full worked example using a fictional PR tool ("Finchling")

## Why this is relevant
Directly applicable to AI-powered SEO content production — shows how practitioners
are measuring and optimizing for AI search visibility, which informs what kind of
content needs to be produced and how to validate its performance in AI search results.
