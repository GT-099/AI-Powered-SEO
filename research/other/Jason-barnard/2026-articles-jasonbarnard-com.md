### 2. The Counterflow Funnel: Search Was Built from the Top Down and Runs from the Bottom Up
- URL: https://jasonbarnard.com/digital-marketing/articles/articles-by/strategy-sandbox/the-counterflow-funnel-search-was-built-from-the-top-down-and-runs-from-the-bottom-up/
- Published: 2026-06-13
- Summary: Argues the marketing funnel never flipped — it was always carrying
  two flows in opposite directions. Search infrastructure is built top-down
  (awareness → conversion) but user behavior runs bottom-up (conversion intent
  → research). Brands must optimize for both flows simultaneously.

### 3. Marketing Builds Backwards: Every Strategy Is Understandability, Credibility, Deliverability, and AIDA Has the Order Upside Down
- URL: https://jasonbarnard.com/digital-marketing/articles/articles-by/strategy-sandbox/marketing-builds-backwards-every-strategy-is-understandability-credibility-deliverability-and-aida-has-the-order-upside-down/
- Published: 2026-06-13
- Summary: Challenges AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action) as starting
  in the wrong place for AI-era marketing. The correct build order is:
  Understandability → Credibility → Deliverability. Brands must be understood
  by machines before they can be trusted, and trusted before they can be
  recommended.

### 4. E-E-A-T Is a Description, Not a Strategy: Credibility Needs a Diagnostic
- URL: https://jasonbarnard.com/digital-marketing/articles/articles-by/strategy-sandbox/e-e-a-t-is-a-description-not-a-strategy-credibility-needs-a-diagnostic/
- Published: 2026-06-13
- Summary: Argues the SEO industry has spent a decade optimizing for E-E-A-T
  without a diagnostic framework. E-E-A-T describes the outcome but doesn't
  tell you what to fix. Credibility needs a diagnostic — a structured way to
  identify which credibility signals are weak and exactly how to address them.

### 5. Your AI Is an Honest Broker: You Brief It to Be Recommended, and Pay It to Be Pushed
- URL: https://jasonbarnard.com/digital-marketing/articles/articles-by/strategy-sandbox/your-ai-is-an-independent-broker/
- Published: June 2026
- Summary: Evolves the "Untrained Salesforce" framework — AI engines are honest
  brokers that can be briefed (through organic content and entity signals) to
  recommend your brand, or paid (through ads) to push it. Brands need both
  strategies working in parallel.

### 6. Everyone's Building a Digital Twin. You Already Have Seven, and They Don't Agree
- URL: https://jasonbarnard.com/digital-marketing/articles/articles-by/strategy-sandbox/everyones-building-a-digital-twin-you-already-have-seven-and-they-dont-agree/
- Published: 2026-06-03
- Summary: Argues every brand already has multiple "digital twins" — the version
  of the brand that exists in ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Google Knowledge
  Graph, Bing, etc. — and they don't agree with each other. The real work is
  achieving consistency across all seven, not building a new one.
