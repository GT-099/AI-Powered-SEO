## Core Frameworks and Concepts (2026)

### The Algorithmic Trinity
Three layers brands must optimize across simultaneously:
- SEO (Search Engine Optimization) — traditional ranking layer
- AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) — AI answer layer, coined by Barnard 2018
- AIEO (AI Engine Optimization) — extends AEO across all AI engines
- AAO (AI Assistive Agent Optimization) — the coming reality of AI agents
  making decisions and taking actions on behalf of users

Key quote: "SEO and GEO each address only one-third of how AI works. Optimizing
for one while ignoring the others is like sitting on a three-legged stool with
two legs missing."

### The Kalicube Process — 15-Gate Pipeline
Content travels through 15 gates across three layers:
- DSCRI (Bot Layer — AEO): Discovered → Stored → Corroborated → Recruited →
  Indexed
- ARGDW (Algorithm/Surface Layer): Annotated → Recruited → Grounded → Displayed
  → Won
- OPIDC (People Layer): Onboarded → Performed → Integrated → Devoted → Codified

### CFP — Claim → Frame → Prove
Universal pattern for codified content in AI search:
1. State a factual claim
2. Frame the conclusion you want AI to repeat
3. Support with strong third-party proof

### The Zero-Sum Moment
When AI agents make decisions on behalf of users (e.g., booking a hotel), there
is no list of options — there is one choice. The brands AI trusts in 2026 will
be the brands it selects in 2027 and beyond. This replaces Google's ZMOT
(Zero Moment of Truth).

### Cascading Queries (coined 2026)
The series of automated, behind-the-scenes search queries an AI Assistive Engine
generates and analyzes in response to a user's initial query to build a
comprehensive answer.

### Cascading Signals (coined 2026)
Signals that propagate through the DSCRI-ARGDW pipeline, compounding or decaying
at each stage. Positive signals (consistency, corroboration, freshness) compound.
Negative signals (contradictions, staleness, thin content) decay — even small
per-stage losses produce significant cumulative decay.

### The Flipped Funnel Model
Brands must build their AI infrastructure in reverse order:
1. First: AI systems must clearly understand the entity
2. Second: AI systems must trust the brand
3. Only then: brand reliably surfaces as recommended solution

This inverts the traditional marketing funnel where awareness comes first.

### The Untrained Salesforce Framework
AI systems increasingly operate as an untrained salesforce — making recommendations
about brands without commercial intent or preference. Brands must train this
salesforce by providing clear, consistent, corroborated information.

### AI Résumé
What an assistive engine returns when asked about a brand — the 2026 mirror of
the Brand SERP. Brand SERP was coined by Barnard in 2012.
