# 20 Years of On-Site SEO Just Lost Half the SERP
- Author: Ross Simmonds

- Published: 2026-06-01

## Summary
Data-backed article arguing that on-site SEO now controls less than half the SERP,
with original research across 309 SERP results and AI Overview citation analysis
across 19 brands.

## Key Data Points

### SERP Real Estate Breakdown (309 results analyzed)
- Organic results: 49%
- AI Overview: 23%
- Video carousel: 14%
- Discussions & forums: 8%
- People Also Ask: 5%
- Local pack: 1%

Conclusion: Organic search now controls less than half the page. The other 51%
belongs to features brands don't write and can't rank in the traditional way.

### AI Overview Citation Sources (19 brands analyzed)
- Reddit: 14 of 19 brands (74%)
- G2: 8 of 19
- Trustpilot: 5 of 19
- YouTube: 5 of 19
- Gartner: 4 of 19
- Capterra: 4 of 19
- PCMag: 3 of 19
- SoftwareAdvice: 3 of 19

Reddit cited in AI Overviews for 74% of brands analyzed — beats G2 nearly 2:1,
beats Gartner more than 3:1.

## Key Arguments

### The New Buyer Journey Starts Before Your Site
Buyers are influenced by SERP features and LLMs before they ever reach a brand's
website. Negative Reddit threads, old G2 reviews, and forum discussions shape AI
answers before any branded content does.

### Reddit Strategy for Fixing SERP Narratives
Playbook used with a $1B brand to flip negative Reddit sentiment:
1. Identify negative sentiment in SERP
2. Create category subreddit and brand subreddit
3. Create persona account and brand account
4. Create content in subreddits
5. Build karma by adding value in relevant threads
6. Create content targeting branded queries
7. Reply to real customers with value
8. Rinse and repeat until SERP flips

### Training Data Problem
The SERP can be outranked but LLM training data cannot be "pushed to page two."
When negative Reddit content gets pulled into model training data, it becomes the
model's memory of the brand — not a ranking problem but a reputation problem
embedded in AI responses.

### Measurement Caveat
Citation tracking is directional, not gospel — SERP features change constantly,
AI Overview citations vary by city and user, and personalization modifies results
per user.
