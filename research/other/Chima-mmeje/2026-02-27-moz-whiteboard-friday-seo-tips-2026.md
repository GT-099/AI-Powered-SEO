## Key Tips Covered (from LinkedIn post corroboration)

### 1. Build Entity Clusters That Cover the Full Query Fan-Out
LLMs like Google AI Mode don't rely on a single search query when generating answers
— they run multiple related queries to build answers from a broader intent set.
Topic clusters help cover full intent for the query while satisfying intent on a
granular level. Framework: Awareness → Comparison → Decision.

### 2. Evolve Reporting Beyond Traffic
As clicks decline, track alternative metrics:
- LLM referrals
- Self-attribution
- Brand demand as influence proxy
- LLM citation depth
- Session quality signals

### 3. Use Community as a Growth Engine
Community strengthens customer bonds, surfaces language your audience uses about
your product, collects feedback, and supports product launches.

### 4. Optimize for Agentic AI and Autonomous Search
Agentic browsing is part of the future of search and conversion. Fix crawl
frictions in conversion paths so bots and agents can crawl pages and take action.
GEO expertise as a technical SEO is particularly valuable here.

### 5. E-E-A-T as Differentiation from Sameness
E-E-A-T is the primary way to stand out from generic AI-generated content.

### 6. Influence Optimization
Narratives and perceptions about your brand are powerful visibility metrics —
building trust and capturing untapped markets through influence optimization.
