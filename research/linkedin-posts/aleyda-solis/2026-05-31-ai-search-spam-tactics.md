Shares a piece by Gianluca Fiorelli warning about manipulative tactics from traditional search reappearing in AI search, with a list of old-to-new spam tactic mappings:
- Cloaking → User-Agent Conditional Prompt Injection
- Doorway Pages/Abuse → RAG Ingestion Flooding / Synthetic Context Stuffing
- Keyword Stuffing & Hidden Text → Semantic/Invisible Prompt Injection
- Link Schemes/Spam → Citation/Co-occurrence Manipulation
- Scraped/Thin Content → AI-Generated Content Spinning (Model Laundering)
- Sneaky Redirects → Citation Hijacking/Dynamic Link Swapping
- Buying Links → LLM Training Dataset Placement (Source Planting)
- And more
