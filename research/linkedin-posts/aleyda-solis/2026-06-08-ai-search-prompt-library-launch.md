## Content
Announcement post for her guide "How to Build a Representative AI Search Prompt Library for Better AI Visibility Measurement." She explains that a common mistake in AI search tracking is using a non-representative prompt library, which can give an inaccurate view of a brand's AI search presence.

A representative prompt library should be a structured sample of AI-assisted journeys relevant to the business — covering products, audiences, markets, journey stages, competitors, and buyer constraints — rather than a random list of prompts or a tool's default set.

The guide covers: what a prompt library is, common mistakes, why representative doesn't mean exhaustive, how to define what the library should represent, how to build/group/localize prompts, how to measure AI search visibility defensibly, and how to turn findings into optimization actions.
