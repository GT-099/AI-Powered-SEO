## Content
The SEO job market has repriced around AI skills — but not just knowing how to use
ChatGPT. Data from 946 full-time SEO roles shows a 27% salary premium for AI-skilled
candidates. Key findings:
- Only 15.5% of SEO postings include AI in the title, but 59.5% require it in the
  description
- Roles with AI in the description capture 4x more listings while still delivering
  a 25% median salary lift
- The premium compounds with seniority
- The market pays for ability to orchestrate AI systems, not just operate tools

Personal note: Kevin uses Cursor and Claude to build Python automation scripts for
data analysis — financial value comes from domain expertise to evaluate AI output,
not from knowing how to prompt.
