## Content
Introduces the concept of "intent compression" caused by Google's AI Overviews (AIO).
For 20 years, search intent (navigational vs comparison) predicted user behavior on
the SERP. AIO erases that signal by pulling every searcher into a reading session
regardless of intent — the brand-name searcher and the product researcher now behave
alike on the SERP page.

## Notes
Key concept for AI-powered SEO content production: if intent no longer predicts
behavior on the SERP, content strategies need to account for this compression when
targeting AI Overview inclusion.
