## Content
Points out that searching for "AI" in job titles misses 4 out of 5 roles that actually
pay more for AI skills. Data breakdown:
- 15.5% of SEO postings include AI in the title
- 59.5% require it somewhere in the description
- Roles with AI in the description deliver 25% median salary lift over non-AI descriptions

Advice: job candidates should screen descriptions not titles; hiring managers should
recognize their pay bands are already two-tier.

## Notes
Companion post to the salary data post — together they paint a picture of how the
