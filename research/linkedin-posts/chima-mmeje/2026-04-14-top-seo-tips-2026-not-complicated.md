## Content
Agrees with Rejoice Ojiaku's statement that "LLM systems are not as complex as
everyone's making them out to be." Shares top SEO tips for 2026 framed as things
practitioners already know — stop overcomplicating AI search:

1. Topic clusters are the best way to organize content and execute full-funnel
2. Internal linking ensures content lives a long, healthy life
3. Community is a growth engine for influencing humans, not machines
4. Optimize for agentic AI and autonomous search — it's happening regardless
5. E-E-A-T is the only way to stand out from "sameness"
6. Influence optimization builds trust and captures untapped market —
   narratives and perceptions about your brand are powerful visibility metrics

## Notes
Strong counterpoint to AI search complexity hype — argues fundamentals still
dominate. The "influence optimization" and "agentic AI optimization" tips are
directly relevant to AI-powered SEO content production strategy.
