## Content
Four detailed SEO tips for 2026:

1. Build entity clusters that cover the full query fan-out — Moz research shows
   LLMs like Google AI Mode run multiple related queries to build answers from a
   broader intent set, not a single search query. Topic clusters help cover full
   intent while satisfying granular-level intent.

2. Evolve reporting beyond traffic — as clicks decline, track alternative metrics:
   LLM referrals, self-attribution, brand demand as influence proxy, LLM citation
   depth, and session quality signals.

3. Use community as a growth engine — for understanding audience language, collecting
   feedback, supporting product launches, and strengthening customer bonds.

4. Optimize for agentic AI and autonomous search — agentic browsing is part of the
   future of search and conversion. Fix crawl frictions in conversion paths so bots
   and agents can crawl pages and take action.
