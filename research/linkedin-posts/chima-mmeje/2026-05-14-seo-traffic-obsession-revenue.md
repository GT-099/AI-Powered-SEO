## Content
Pushes back against the SEO industry habit of reporting on traffic dips for popular
brands. Argues that the obsession with traffic ignores the bigger picture of revenue
— a brand that is profitable despite a traffic dip is still succeeding. Calls out
the pattern of targeting HubSpot, ClickUp, and others for traffic drops while
ignoring their continued profitability.

Calls for the industry to instead tell stories of brands driving revenue with SEO
and scaling year-over-year — a more constructive and business-relevant narrative.

## Notes
Important perspective shift for AI-powered SEO content production — argues that
content teams need to evolve beyond traffic as the primary success metric and focus
on revenue impact instead. Relevant to the reporting evolution discussion.
