## Content
Describes the "invisible research phase" in modern B2B buying: prospects book demos
already informed, with specific questions and pre-formed opinions — increasingly from
AI tools that summarized the category, compared players, and shaped a point of view
before any human conversation. Sales teams get credit for closing but AI got there
first.

If a brand isn't showing up in AI research phase, every sales conversation starts
at a disadvantage.
