## Content
Argues AI assistants influence purchasing decisions before buyers reach a website —
evaluating brands based on post-sale evidence: onboarding experiences, measurable
outcomes, client advocacy. Most of that evidence exists in CRMs, support tickets,
customer success calls, and QBRs — but is invisible to machines.

Introduces the OPIDC framework:
- Onboarded
- Performed
- Integrated
- Devoted
- Codified

The first four stages are just running a business. Codified is what most companies
skip — and what determines whether AI recommends you or a competitor.

Client example: sales team mostly fills in onboarding forms now because AI has
already done the selling before the call starts. Inquiry volume down. Revenue up.
Buyers arriving already convinced.
