## Content
Argues most brands are fixing the wrong problem in AI search — thinking they
need more content when AI search is actually a multiplicative system where one
weak signal limits everything downstream.

You can have great content, strong SEO, and brilliant positioning — but if
engines can't confidently understand your entity, connect claims to proof, or
frame your brand correctly, you won't win the recommendation.

Key insight: "Your weakest gate sets your ceiling." Most companies improve their
A grades while ignoring the F grade killing their entire pipeline. Highest-leverage
work is almost never "create more" — it's fixing the earliest broken gate,
connecting existing claims to existing proof, and making brand narrative
machine-understandable. Most assets already exist — the opportunity is joining
the dots.

## Notes
Critical counterpoint to "publish more content" strategies — directly challenges
the quantity-first approach to AI-powered SEO content production. The "weakest
gate" framework is a useful diagnostic tool for content audits.
