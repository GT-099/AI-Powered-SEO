## Content
Argues that AI Assistive Engines are literal — they don't figure out brand
positioning, they follow what brands give them. Brands must build the bridge
between claims and the commercially powerful conclusion they want AI to repeat.

Introduces the Claim-Frame-Prove methodology:
1. State a factual claim
2. Frame the conclusion you want AI to repeat
3. Support that claim with strong third-party proof

This turns AI into a brand advocate — repeating a frame the brand engineered.
When brands leave gaps in their digital footprint, AI defaults to safe,
non-committal conclusions that benefit competitors who frame their narrative properly.

## Notes
Most actionable framework in the Jason Barnard collection — Claim-Frame-Prove
is a directly implementable content production methodology for AI-powered SEO.
Each piece of content should be built around this three-part structure.
