## Content
Addresses the challenge of measuring AI brand recommendations — argues no one
has truly cracked this yet and clean dashboards tracking visibility across
ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Mode are "best guesses dressed up as metrics."

Introduces the Funnel Query Pathway framework (borrowed from macroeconomics,
not SEO):

1. Pick your most profitable customer at the moment they're ready to buy
2. Write the exact query they'd type with your brand name — this is the
   conversion node
3. Project upward — evaluation questions, awareness questions — until you have
   a 50-60 query tree mapping the full customer journey
4. Run the tree monthly across every AI engine

Unlike keyword research, this asks what your ideal cohort WOULD ask given a
specific intent — not what people actually type.

One tree does three jobs: Strategy (what content to create), Measurement (monthly
AI engine tracking), Analysis (spot gaps, track momentum, adjust).
