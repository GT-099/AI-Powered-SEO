## Content
Reports a trend of decreasing branded click-through rates from Google, based on
internal SEO data from large publishers. References Kevin Indig and Eric Van
Buskirk's analysis of 846,000 sessions showing users spend significantly more time
on Google itself when AI Overviews are present for branded/navigational searches.

Three conclusions for publishers:
1. Google is the clear winner — users get more information about brands from Google
   itself without clicking through
2. Publishers should expect continued decreasing CTRs from branded search as AI
   Overviews/AI Mode evolve
3. If users spend more time reading AI answers about your brand on Google, it becomes
   critical to ensure AI Overviews contain accurate and compelling brand information

## Notes
Important for AI-powered SEO content production — even branded search is now
affected by AI Overview content. Brand narrative management in AI responses becomes
as important as traditional SEO.
