## Content
Shares Amanda Natividad's framing: SEO is becoming a "Public Evidence" game.
Monthly SEO reports still narrowly focus on organic traffic, confusing traffic
decline with demand decline. Demand is created everywhere — Reddit, news, email,
Slack — sources that may not show up in analytics but influence clicks and
conversions.

Key quote shared: "We're moving away from an era of 'rank my page' to an era of
'influence the public record.'"

## Notes
Strong conceptual reframe for AI-powered SEO content production — "influencing
the public record" rather than ranking pages directly maps to how AI citations
work. Content strategy needs to shift toward evidence creation across all public
channels.
