## Content
Shares Chartbeat data showing SEO traffic decline is NOT evenly distributed —
smallest publishers are hardest hit:
- 60% decline for small publishers (1-10,000 daily views)
- 47% decline for medium publishers (10,000-100,000 daily views)
- 22% decline for large publishers (100,000+ daily views)

Notes Chartbeat primarily supports media and content-driven websites — the exact
types most reliant on SEO and most affected by AI answers.

## Notes
Critical data point for AI-powered SEO content production — smaller content
operations face disproportionately larger challenges. Content strategy needs to
account for scale disadvantages when competing against large publishers for AI
citations.
