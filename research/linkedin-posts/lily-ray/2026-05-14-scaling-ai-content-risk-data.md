## Content
Shares alarming data from monitoring 220+ sites that AI content platforms publicly
named as customer success stories:
- 54% have lost at least 30% of their peak organic traffic
- 22% have lost 75% or more of their peak organic traffic

Pattern observed: big content scaling push → short-lived traffic spike → steep drop
a few months later (often right after the case study goes live).

Draws parallel to the Helpful Content Update and March 2024 Core Update cycle —
argues much of what is being sold as "AI-first SEO" or "GEO at scale" looks
remarkably similar to what caused those earlier site crashes.

## Notes
Most data-heavy post in this collection — the 220+ site monitoring study with 54%/22%
traffic loss figures is a critical reference point for understanding the risks of
low-quality AI content scaling. Essential counterpoint to "publish more AI content"
advice common in the industry.
