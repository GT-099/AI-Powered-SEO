## Content
Observes LinkedIn building curated "top content" pages aggregating top-performing
posts by topic (e.g., /top-content/ folder) — essentially programmatic UGC category
pages. The folder is already gaining significant search visibility, ranking #1 for
queries like "Helpful Content Update."

Notes that programmatic UGC pages work differently at scale depending on content
originality, page volume, and user experience — and that large platforms like LinkedIn
can get away with approaches that would be much riskier for smaller sites.

## Notes
Relevant observation for AI-powered SEO content production — shows how platforms
are using AI-assisted content aggregation for programmatic SEO, and highlights the
double standard between large and small sites in Google's evaluation.
