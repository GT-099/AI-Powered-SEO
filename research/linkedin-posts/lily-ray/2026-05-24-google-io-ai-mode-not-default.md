## Content
Live recap of Google IO 2026 search updates. Key announcements:
- AI Mode is NOT becoming the default search experience (yet) — relief for many SEOs
- "Google search is AI search" — 1 billion facts updated every minute
- Search box now uses AI to help users ask better questions (beyond autocomplete)
- Follow-up questions take users from AI Overviews into AI Mode (rolling out globally)
- Search bar now accepts images, live camera shots, and uploaded files
- Google incorporating agents in search — complex questions mapped into plans with
  automatic notifications even outside the search interface
- Generative UI from Antigravity coming to Search in summer 2026

Her take: AI Mode not becoming default is a relief, but new features will continue
cutting into organic traffic. Conversion rates may improve for retailers, ecommerce,
and local businesses as results get more personalized.
