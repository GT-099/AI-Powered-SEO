## Content
Shares a practical hack for converting SEO keywords into AI prompts at scale using
Google Sheets' =AI() or =Gemini() function. Takes keywords with real search volume
(from Ahrefs, Semrush, Similarweb) and converts them into natural-language queries
that users would type into AI assistants like ChatGPT, Gemini, or voice assistants.

Prompt used:
"You are converting a terse SEO search keyword into the natural-language query a real
person would actually type or speak to an AI assistant. Rewrite the keyword as a single
conversational question or request that preserves the exact search intent and intent
type (informational, commercial, transactional, or local). Phrase it the way someone
would genuinely ask out loud in a full sentence — not in keyword shorthand."

## Notes
Directly applicable to AI-powered SEO content production — bridges traditional keyword
research with AI prompt targeting. A scalable, free method using Google Sheets native
AI functions, no coding required.
