## Content
Argues that destroying your SEO destroys your AI search visibility — sites that lose
Google visibility quickly lose ChatGPT citations shortly after. Many GEO tactics being
recommended are actually bad for SEO long-term.

Key points:
- New GEO practitioners often lack the years of SEO experience needed to distinguish
  sustainable tactics from short-lived loopholes
- A gold rush of AI search manipulation tactics has worked short-term but is now
  catching up with sites algorithmically (crashes, manual actions)
- Google employees are publicly discouraging "inauthentic mentions" and GEO hacks
- LLMs like ChatGPT and Claude are actively taking steps to mitigate spam in citations
- Best approach: build a brand that gets cited after AI companies clean up spam

## Notes
Critical counterpoint for AI-powered SEO content production — warns against
short-term GEO manipulation tactics. The data on sites losing visibility post-spam
is directly relevant to understanding what sustainable AI content strategies look like.
