## Content
Argues AI Search is inherently biased — editorially biased based on how it was
trained and built, and query-biased based on how users phrase questions. People
subconsciously want biased answers that confirm their existing preferences.

Key implication: as marketers, understanding that AI Search is editorial and biased
should guide brand positioning, content strategy, and SEO — content needs to align
with how the model was trained to think about topics, not just keyword match.

## Notes
Foundational framing post — shorter but philosophically important for AI-powered
SEO content production. The "AI Search is editorial" framing reframes content
strategy from keyword targeting to model alignment.
