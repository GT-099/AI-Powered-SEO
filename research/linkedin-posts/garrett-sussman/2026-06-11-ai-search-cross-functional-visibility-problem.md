## Content
Based on three months of discovery calls across companies at different stages of
AI Search adoption. Key observation: every company has a different context —
resources, teams, planning stages, content foundations, leadership pressure, and
opinions on channel value.

Breaks down AI Search challenges by role:
- Ecommerce SEO lead: cross-functional ownership problem (SEO, content, product feed,
  merchandising, PR, dev, CX all involved)
- Newsroom SEO lead: site structure, author pages, crawlability, explaining AI to
  editorial without making it feel like a stain on journalism
- Lean marketing team: prioritization problem — what moves visibility in 30-60 days?
- Mid-market CMO: deciding whether to hand AI Search to SEO without dedicated budget

Conclusion: A responsible AI Search program needs ownership, resourcing, internal
education, agreed-upon metrics, and clear revenue connection. Treat it as a
cross-functional visibility problem, not a side project for SEO.
