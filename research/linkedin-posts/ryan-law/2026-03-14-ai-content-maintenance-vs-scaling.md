## Content
Broke/Woke framing on AI content strategy:
- Broke: using AI to scale SEO content creation
- Woke: using AI to automate content maintenance so you can focus on scaling
  research, experiments, and editorial content

## Notes
Short but strategically important reframe — shifts AI content production from
"create more" to "maintain existing + free humans for higher-value work." Directly
challenges the dominant "use AI to publish more content" narrative.
