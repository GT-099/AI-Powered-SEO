## Content
Argues that until recently AI content wasn't good enough for SEO — but now it is.
Generative AI can now create content indistinguishable from the vast corpus of
human-written content produced by content marketers. AI has become a more thorough
researcher, more compliant with brand/voice guidelines, more flexible in response
to feedback, faster, and more efficient. There is no longer an inherent trade-off
in using AI for content creation.

Frames this as a necessary honest conversation — the path forward requires admitting
where AI can and should be used, so humans can focus on parts of marketing where
they'll have a longer, happier tenure.

## Notes
One of the most direct practitioner statements on AI content quality reaching parity
with human content — directly relevant to AI-powered SEO content production. The
"no longer a trade-off" claim is significant coming from a content strategist at Ahrefs.
