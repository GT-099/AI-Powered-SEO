## Content
Argues the open web is dying — not slowing, not changing, but dying. Cites Cloudflare
data showing AI bot traffic grew 187% last year while human traffic grew only 3.1%.
Coins the term "Machine Media" to describe the new era where machines are the primary
consumers of web content.

## Notes
Highly relevant to AI-powered SEO content production — directly reframes who content
is being written for (machines vs humans). The "Machine Media" concept is a core
framework for understanding why AI-powered SEO content strategies are necessary.
