## Content
Pushes back against the "it's just SEO" argument that dismisses GEO as a separate
discipline. Key counterpoints made:

1. Even where SEO and GEO tactics overlap, the value exchange is different — just
   like PR, link building, fundraising, and outbound sales use similar tactics but
   are treated as separate budget line items and teams.

2. SEO has not kept up with advances in retrieval. Most legacy SEO tools give insights
   based on old paradigms that don't align with modern information retrieval.

3. Data shows traditional SEO optimization alone is insufficient for AI surfaces:
   - ZipTie.dev data: being in Google top 10 gives only 25% chance of being cited
     by AI surfaces
   - Profound data: only 19% overlap between Google Search and ChatGPT visibility
   - Josh Blyskal data: AI citation is more about relevance than position

## Notes
Strong data-backed argument for why AI-powered SEO content production requires a
different strategic approach than traditional SEO. The cited statistics (25% top-10
overlap, 19% Google/ChatGPT visibility overlap) are useful research reference points.
